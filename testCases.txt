Java Calculator Assignment 1 Test Cases

Test Case 1: Addition
Input:
First Number: 5
Second Number: 3
Operation: Addition (1)
Expected Output:
Result: 5 + 3 = 8
Observations:
The calculator correctly adds the two numbers and displays the result.

Test Case 2: Subtraction
Input:
First Number: 10
Second Number: 4
Operation: Subtraction (2)
Expected Output:
Result: 10 - 4 = 6
Observations:
The calculator correctly subtracts the second number from the first and displays the result.

Input:
First Number: 6
Second Number: 2
Operation: Multiplication (3)
Expected Output:
Result: 6 * 2 = 12
Observations:
The calculator correctly multiplies the two numbers and displays the result.

Test Case 4: Division
Input:
First Number: 20
Second Number: 5
Operation: Division (4)
Expected Output:
Result: 20 / 5 = 4
Observations:
The calculator correctly divides the first number by the second and displays the result.
